What is a Cone?

A cone appears to be a 3D shape with a circular base that is pointed upwards like a spike with one point at the end.

Properties:

Faces:
The cone has two faces, one circular and one curved.

Edges:
The cone has one edge being the rim of the circular face.

Vertices:
The cone has one vertex being at the peak of the cone.
